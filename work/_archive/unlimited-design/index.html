Brand Identity

Unlimited Design Co.

Brand identity and marketing suite for Unlimited Design Co.—a design studio serving real estate agents and local businesses in the San Gabriel Valley. Logo, stationery, folders, and templates that feel editorial, feminine, and ready to hand a client on day one.

Logo & Visual Identity

The orchid mark anchors the brand—a stylized flower with a UDC monogram at its center, drawn in gold and sage with dusty rose accents. It reads botanical and premium without feeling fussy, and scales cleanly from social avatars to foil-stamped folder covers.

Script “Unlimited” pairs with spaced caps for “Design Company,” finished with the tagline: Boundless creativity, unlimited potential.

Realtor Marketing Suite

The flagship offer is a full client-ready suite—not a single logo file. Sample work for Audrey Chen Real Estate shows what a new agent receives: bio cards, listing showcases, service brochures, property grids, and investment spreads, all in a cohesive navy-and-gold system.

A companion listing booklet cover demonstrates how the same visual language extends to longer-form print pieces agents can leave behind after an open house.

Social Presence

The Facebook cover carries the full identity into digital space—orchid illustration, script wordmark, and tagline sized for the banner crop, so the studio looks as polished online as it does in print.
