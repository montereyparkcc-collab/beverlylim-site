About

Licensed product, apparel, and community.

I'm a senior graphic designer and illustrator with over a decade of experience turning briefs into bold, market-ready visuals—from licensed bag collections at Loungefly and Funko to textile graphics for national retailers.

Today I bring that same editorial eye to creative direction for the Monterey Park Chamber of Commerce and brand work through Unlimited Design Co. My work is confident, feminine, and never corporate.
